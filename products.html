Material Concept

Three Natural Materials

Stone

Base egg carton / No additive

The natural colour of egg carton pulp as-is. A grey-brown texture that forms naturally after drying. The true character of the material, with nothing added.

Moss

+ Green Tea Powder

Dried green tea powder blended into the pulp. Natural pigment lends a soft green hue, and fine tea fibres add depth to the texture.

Soil

+ Coffee Grounds

Used coffee grounds from drip brewing blended into the pulp. A deep brown tone and the coarse, gritty texture of coffee create a surface that resembles soil.
