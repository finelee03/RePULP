How to Add Your Photos & Videos

Place your image or video files in the assets/ folder, then update the src attribute of each slot. Placeholder tiles will disappear automatically once a valid source is set.

  • 1
    Create an assets/ folder inside the RePULP/ directory
  • 2
    Copy your .jpg / .png / .mp4 files into it
  • 3
    In lab.html, set src="assets/your-file.jpg" on the relevant <img>
  • 4
    For video: use <video src="assets/file.mp4" controls> or paste a YouTube <iframe>
  • 5
    Commit & push — GitHub Pages updates automatically
